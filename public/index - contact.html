Simplx

Introduction

Simplx is a cloud-based software application for the structural design of buildings. With unparalleled modelling efficiency, you can perform fast and easy design iterations through intelligent design and analysis automation. Work independently or collaboratively with development teams and project consultants.

Automate

Automate analysis model creation and execution. Streamline post-processing for rapid and actionable insights.

Eliminate

Eliminate redundant inputs by maximising reuse. Test assumptions and scenarios without duplicating models.

Collaborate

Collaborate with team members and invite contributors and reviewers. Avoid stale data and error-prone data transfers.

Simplx in Detail

Get creative and leave the time-consuming and repetitive modelling tasks to Simplx.

Designed from the ground up with prefabrication in mind, Simplx offers a suite of tools to prepare detailed models of buildings which are an assembly of components such as mass-timber, precast concrete, steel and hybrid structures. Of course, Simplx supports cast in-situ concrete as well.

Simplx automates finicky and error-prone tasks such as part and assembly definition, mesh compatibility and connector and constraint assignment.

Web Tools

The cloud-native Simplx application brings flexible, high performance computing and data management to property design and development teams.

The clean and simple web user interface lets you build detailed 3D analysis models in minutes, not days. Built for scale, you can execute dozens of analyses in parallel, meaning you get results sooner without the headache of orchestrating and managing serial execution.

Our application programming interface (API) enables bolt-on advanced design and analysis capability to support your automation journey. Embed Simplx into your PropTech application to achieve design certainty sooner.

Generative Design Engine

Optioneering and value engineering are made easy by allowing designers to input design goals and constraints. Simplx explores possible permutations of a solution to find the best design option.

Easily compare multiple schemes and choose the metrics that matter most to quantify your design decisions.

Physics Engine

AI/ML is disrupting AEC, but it only takes you so far. When life-safety is at stake, it would be crazy not validate your designs with a physics-based analysis.

Validation and verification are achieved through a suite of tools letting you overlay simple idealisations and analytical expressions with advanced 3D numerical modelling techniques.
